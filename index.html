Frequently asked

What is the Gadehi caste / surname?

Gadehi (also spelled Gadhi) is a community and surname. This page is maintained by community members to document its history, culture, and notable people as verified information is gathered.

Is Gadhi the same as Gadehi?

Gadhi is a common alternate spelling of Gadehi, arising from regional transliteration differences. This page treats them as the same community.
